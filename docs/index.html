How It Works

Simple. Private. Intelligent.

One command to get started. AI that understands your documents. Controlled access for those who need it.

1

Create your vault

Run soul-legacy init or sign up for managed hosting. A guided wizard walks you through setup. Your vault is encrypted locally before anything is stored.

2

Add records and ingest documents

Add structured records (assets, insurance, contacts) or upload documents directly. PDFs, scanned images, and Word docs are automatically extracted, chunked, and indexed for AI retrieval.

3

Ask questions in plain English

Chat with your estate data. Soul Legacy retrieves relevant document excerpts and structured records, then answers with citations — "per your will.pdf, page 3..."

4

Grant controlled access to those who need it

Give your attorney access to only legal documents. Give your accountant access to assets and debts. When the time comes, your executor gets a guided, AI-assisted walkthrough of your complete estate.

Security

Zero-knowledge. Always.

We can't read your data. Not because we choose not to — because it's mathematically impossible without your passphrase.

🔐

AES encryption

All data encrypted with AES-128-CBC + HMAC-SHA256. Key derived via PBKDF2-SHA256 with 600,000 iterations — OWASP 2023 standard.

🏠

Local-first

Free tier stores everything on your device. No data leaves unless you choose cloud backup. Your key never does.

🔗

Blockchain anchoring

Vault fingerprint committed to Polygon — proves your documents haven't been tampered with since signing.

📖

Open core

Encryption layer is open source. Audit it yourself. No trust required — verify the math.

🗝️

Partial access keys

Scoped keys let you grant access to specific sections only. Your attorney sees legal docs. Your accountant sees financials. Nothing more.

⏰

Dead man's switch

Configurable check-in interval. If you don't check in, encrypted access instructions are sent to your designated contacts.
